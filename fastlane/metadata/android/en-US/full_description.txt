The Strings-Manager is android application for help developers to create string quickly 

<p>Features</p>

- Create and Edit: You can  create & edit Strings
- Save: you can save the strings
- Open source: You can see how the app works for yourself! 
- Material Design 3: Modern and elegant design for your comfort! 
- Navigation: Simple and intuitive navigation 
- Support: Support for Landscape and Portrait!
